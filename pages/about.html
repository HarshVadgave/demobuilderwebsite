Our Story

Built by engineers. Backed by clients. Trusted by generations.

Founded in 2005 by a small team of civil engineers, Demo Builders began with a single conviction: construction should be honest work — measured, documented, and delivered on time. Twenty years later, that founding conviction is the same lens we apply to a ₹5 lakh interior fit-out or a ₹640 crore township.

Today we are 200+ engineers, architects, project managers and skilled tradespeople, spread across active sites in Pune, Mumbai, Nashik and the greater Konkan belt. What has not changed is our process discipline — the same six-stage delivery model applies to every project we sign.

Vision, mission & values

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in construction and real-estate development across western India — measured by the number of clients who return.

Our Mission

Deliver every project on time, on budget and above specification — with fully documented safety, quality and compliance at every stage.

Our Values

Integrity in billing. Zero-compromise safety. Craftsmanship that outlives fashion. Transparency in every contract.

Chairman's Message

"We measure our success in referrals, not launches."

When we started Demo in 2005, our promise was simple — build it right, deliver it on time, and never dilute quality to protect margin. Two decades and 240 projects later, that promise is the reason 70% of our new business comes from client referrals. We remain, and will always remain, an engineer-led firm.
