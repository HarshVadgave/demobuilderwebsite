Who We Are

Two decades of building structures that stand the test of time

Demo Builders was founded in 2005 with a simple promise: build it right, deliver it on time, and never compromise on safety or quality. What began as a small residential contracting firm has grown into one of the region's most trusted names in construction and real-estate development.

Today, our multidisciplinary teams of engineers, architects and project managers handle everything from luxury residential towers to large-scale infrastructure works — backed by transparent processes and government-approved compliance at every stage.
