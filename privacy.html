Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, store and protect the personal information you share with us.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Demo Builders ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses and safeguards information you provide through our website, forms and phone communications. By using this site you consent to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information you submit through enquiry forms (name, phone, email, project details).
  • Basic technical information (browser type, device, referring page) collected via standard web analytics.
  • Any additional information you voluntarily share during conversations with our sales or project teams.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your project enquiries and schedule consultations.
  • To send updates about ongoing projects or new launches, only if you have opted in.
  • To improve our website content and understand which pages are most useful.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with (a) our internal delivery teams, (b) statutory bodies where required by law, and (c) third-party service providers (e.g. email delivery) under strict confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Retention

We retain enquiry data for as long as it is needed to serve you, and for statutory record-keeping thereafter. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time.

5. Cookies

Our website uses standard cookies for basic session functionality and anonymous analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser without losing access to the site's main features.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to request deletion of your data.
  • Right to withdraw consent for marketing communications.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information we hold. No system is 100% secure — please avoid sharing highly sensitive information through public forms.

8. Contact

Questions or requests regarding this policy can be sent to hello@demobuilders.example or by post to our Vasai-Virar office.
